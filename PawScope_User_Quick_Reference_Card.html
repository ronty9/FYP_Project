🚀 Getting Started
  • Sign Up / Login — Create an account using Email/Password, or use Google Sign-In for quick access.
  • Create a Pet Profile — Head to the Profile tab and tap "Add Pet" to set up your companion.
  • Customize Profile — Add a cute photo, assign their breed, weight, gender, and birthdate.
  • OTP Security — You may be requested to verify your account via OTP for security purposes.
💡 Tip: You can manage multiple pet profiles under a single user account.
🐕 AI Breed Scanner
  • 1. Select Species — Choose either 'Cat' or 'Dog' before scanning.
  • 2. Capture Photo — Use your camera live or upload a recent photo from your gallery.
  • 3. Analyze — Tap the scan button and let PawScope process the image.
  • 4. View Results — Find out your pet's breed! The app displays the top-5 likely matches with confidence %.
📸 Best Results: Ensure well-lit photos showing the pet's full face and body.
🩺 AI Skin Disease Scanner
  • Purpose — Analyze unusual skin patches, rashes, or lesions on your pet.
  • How To — Select the proper species, take a clear & close-up photo of the affected area, and begin the scan.
  • Results — View the Top-3 potential skin conditions and access general advice.
  • History — Your scans are saved in your Scan History so you can review them during a vet visit.
⚠ Disclaimer: PawScope provides AI-assisted analysis and should NEVER replace professional medical advice. Always consult a certified veterinarian for diagnosis and medical treatment.

PawScope Features & Support

End User Guide
📱 System Requirements
  • Hardware — iOS or Android smartphone with a functional camera.
  • Internet Connection — Active Wi-Fi or cellular data connection required for AI scanning and cloud syncing.
  • Software OS — Android 6.0+ (Marshmallow) or iOS 12.0+.
  • Device Permissions — Camera, Gallery/Storage, and Push Notifications must be enabled for full functionality.
🦴 Pet Management & Gallery
  • Pet Details — Track basic information like age, weight progression, and assigned breed.
  • Photo Gallery — Tap into a pet's profile to create a dedicated photo gallery of all their best moments.
  • Editing Details — You can easily update your pet's details anytime via the Profile tab.
  • Removal — Delete pet profiles if they are no longer needed (Warning: This will also remove their gallery photos).
⏰ Schedules & Reminders
  • Categories — Organize tasks by specific tags: Grooming, Medication, Vaccine, Food/Water, or Custom tasks.
  • Calendar View — Head to the Calendar tab to see all upcoming tasks sorted by date for easy viewing.
  • Push Notifications — Never miss a task! Get local push alerts right on your phone when a reminder is due.
  • Customization — Adjust notification preferences (Sound / Vibration) within the Profile settings menu.
🔔 Note: Be sure to grant "Notification" permissions to PawScope in your phone's OS settings to receive reminders.
🤝 Community & Support
  • Community Tips — Convenient access to curated articles and tips on pet care, diet, and training right on your Home dashboard.
  • FAQs — Browse the frequently asked questions to learn more about application features and pet care basics.
  • Submit Feedback — Found a bug or have a suggestion? Use the feedback form! Application admins can review and reply directly to you.
  • Password Resets — Forgotten your password? Securely reset it right from the login screen using your registered email.
🛠️ Pro Tips & Troubleshooting
Issue / GoalRecommendation
Low AI Confidence Scores Ensure the room is well-lit. Avoid blurry photos, fast-moving subjects, or cluttered backgrounds. Focus directly on the pet's face/body or the specific skin area.
Inaccurate / Confused Results Always double check your species selection! The AI models are strictly categorized by species. Running a cat photo through the dog AI will yield incorrect results.
Missing Push Notifications Verify your phone's System Settings → Notifications → PawScope and ensure alerts are allowed. Finally, confirm your Sound/Vibe settings in the app.
Reviewing Past Scans Need to show your vet a scan you did last month? Navigate to your Profile tab and tap Scan History to view the full log of all your AI scan results.
